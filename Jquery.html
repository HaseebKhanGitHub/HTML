Information Technology

What is Information Technology?

Information Technology (IT) refers to the use, development, and management of computer systems, software, networks, and electronic data. It involves the study, design, implementation, support, and management of computer-based information systems.

Importance of IT

IT plays a crucial role in various aspects of modern life, including business operations, communication, data storage and analysis, entertainment, education, healthcare, and more. It enables automation, improves efficiency, facilitates communication, and drives innovation across industries.
